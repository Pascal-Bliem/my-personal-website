My short Bio

I'm currently employed as a Data Scientist at HDI, one of Germany's largest insurance companies, where I work on customer value models, churn prevention, marketing support, and data infrastructure improvements.

Between my graduation and starting my current job, I spent over a year backpacking through 10 southeast Asian countries, staying significant portions of the time in Vietnam and Indonesia, where I volunteered as a teacher. During my travels, I got the chance to work on several personal projects to sharpen my data science and coding skills, as I was transitioning to the field from a background of academic research.

2018 I graduated summa cum laude with a master degree in materials engineering from RWTH Aachen University, where I had also been working in materials chemistry and phyiscs reasearch, supplemented by research stays at National Tsing Hua University and Uppsala University.

My research focused mostly on experimental and computational investigation of synthesis and characterization of nano-scaled materials and thin films for applications in energy generaion, electronics, and wear resistance.
